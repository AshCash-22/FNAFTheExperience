Freddy Fazbear is the main character for the Five Nights at Freddy’s franchise (hence the name). Freddy is an animatronic bear and the star attraction of the original Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, opened in 1983, as well as the face and namesake of the company that owns it. Freddy is heavily implied to be possessed by the restless spirit of Gabriel, a little boy murdered by William Afton.

Foxy the Pirate, also known as Foxy, is one of the four original animatronics of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza and a major antagonist in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. Foxy is a discontinued animatronic pirate fox entertainer. He resides at his own separate stage in the pizzeria. Undisclosed to Fazbear Entertainment, Inc. and the public.

Bonnie the Rabbit is one of the four original animatronics of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Bonnie is an animatronic rabbit and the guitarist in Freddy's band, positioned on the left side of the stage. At Fazbear Entertainment, Inc. and the public, Bonnie is heavily implied to be possessed by the restless spirit of Jeremy, a little boy murdered by William Afton. Due to this, he and the others are now seeking revenge against their killer by attacking any adults in the pizzeria after hours in a blind rage.

Chica the Chicken, also known as Chica, is one of the four original animatronics of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza and a major antagonist in the Five Nights at Freddy's series. Chica is an animatronic chicken and the backup singer in Freddy's band, positioned at the right side of the stage. Undisclosed to Fazbear Entertainment, Inc. and the public, Chica is possessed by the restless spirit of Susie – a little girl murdered by William Afton.
